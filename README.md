# The Wild Monkey - Writing Portfolio

A beautifully crafted fable by Donatus Prosper Ihechi about wisdom, leadership, and fairness.

## Story Overview

**Title:** The Wild Monkey  
**Author:** Donatus Prosper Ihechi  
**Published:** September 2025  
**Genre:** Fable / Short Story

### Summary

This timeless tale follows Moki, a clever monkey in the jungle of Kairu, as he challenges the reign of King Zubari, the mighty lion. Through wisdom and service rather than strength, Moki proves that true leadership lies in fairness and integrity.

### Core Lesson

> **True leadership is not about strength, but wisdom, fairness, and service.**
